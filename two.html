About Us

Welcome to Penny Arcade, the ultimate all-you-can-play arcade experience right here in the mall! At Penny Arcade, we’re dedicated to delivering endless fun and excitement for gamers of all ages. Our vibrant, modern space is packed with a diverse selection of games, from nostalgic classics to cutting-edge new releases. With our all-inclusive play model, you can enjoy unlimited access to every game without worrying about extra coins or tokens. Whether you’re challenging friends, enjoying family time, or just having a blast on your own, Penny Arcade offers a fantastic atmosphere and unbeatable value. Step into a world of fun where the only limit is your imagination—at Penny Arcade, the good times never stop!
